ABOUT HACKRUSH

  • JOIN US!

Crazy as it seems is what we all call the HackRush hackathon that goes down every Friday Evening till Dawn. This is an intense weekend of learning, building and sharing cool stuff.

Whether you are an expert or novice, developer or designer, join us every Friday evening at the DeHUB for what we guarantee will be a memorable experience.
